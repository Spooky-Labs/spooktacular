Where competitors are ahead

We believe in earning trust through honesty. Here is where others have advantages today.

Tart

Established OCI image ecosystem

Tart has a mature library of pre-built OCI images with multiple Xcode versions. Spooktacular's OCI support is on the roadmap but not yet shipped. If you need pre-built images today, Tart has the edge.

Anka

Longer production track record

Anka has been used in enterprise CI/CD for years. They have refined error handling, edge case coverage, and battle-tested reliability that comes from time in production. Spooktacular is newer.

Orka

Fully managed, zero ops

If you want zero operational burden, Orka handles everything: hardware, networking, updates, monitoring. Spooktacular is self-hosted, which means you manage the infrastructure.

Anka & Orka

Enterprise support with SLAs

Both Anka and Orka offer paid enterprise support with contractual SLAs, dedicated account managers, and phone support. Spooktacular currently offers community support via GitHub.

When to choose what

Honest guidance for every team shape and budget.

Choose Spooktacular

Best total value, zero lock-in

You want a native GUI + CLI + API, Kubernetes integration, EC2 autoscaling, and transparent pricing. You value open source and are comfortable with community support. Your budget is better spent on hardware, not software licenses.

Choose Tart

Proven CLI-only, established ecosystem

You only need CLI-based VM management without a GUI. You're already using Cirrus CI or want Tart's established OCI image ecosystem. Budget for Orchard if you need orchestration beyond basic CLI.

Choose Anka

Enterprise sales relationship required

Your procurement process requires a vendor relationship with enterprise support SLAs. You need a mature, commercially supported product and budget is not a primary concern. You prefer proprietary vendor backing.

Choose Orka

Fully managed, no hardware to own

You don't want to manage Mac hardware at all. You prefer a fully managed cloud solution and are comfortable with MacStadium lock-in. Willing to pay a premium for zero operational burden.
